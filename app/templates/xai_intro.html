Explainable AI (XAI) in Heart Disease Prediction

Our heart disease prediction model is powered by Explainable AI (XAI) to ensure transparency and trust. We use SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) to explain how our model makes predictions, providing insights into the contribution of each feature towards the result.

Why Explainable AI?

  • Enhances model transparency and accountability.
  • Helps understand the impact of individual features.
  • Builds trust with users through clear explanations.
